About this app

Features of this app:

- Apply for leave

- Apply for an exit permit

- View your paystubss

- Manager approvals

- Message Center

- Support for Apple Watch

For iOS users:

Please ensure you follow these steps after you install the app:

1. Go to Settings > General > Profiles & Device Management

2. Click on "Al Jazeera Satellite Network" under enterprise apps

3. Click on "Trust Al Jazeera Satellite Network"

4. Click on "Trust”
